About Me

I'm a Mobile Solution Architect with a passion for creating intuitive digital experiences that blend technical excellence with exceptional user experience.

With over 7 years of experience in mobile development, I've mastered both iOS and Android platforms, specializing in Swift and Kotlin. My background in architecture allows me to bring a holistic approach to turning complex ideas into polished, user-friendly applications.

Currently working at Bosch Digital, I architect mobile solutions that combine innovation with practicality. I'm also an entrepreneur running GMGStudio, where I develop mobile games, design engaging experiences, and produce creative content.

When I'm not coding, I explore the local culinary scene as StuttgartFoodie Alex—discovering hidden gems and sharing my experiences with fellow food enthusiasts.
